ASMR Arcade

An arcade full of satisfying little machines: pop, rake, squish, pour and play. No ads, no subscription — just calm.

The machines

  • Pop-It, Classic Wrap & Glass Bubbles — three kinds of bubble popping
  • Zen Garden — rake, smooth, and decorate real-feeling sand
  • Kinetic Sand, Sand Gravity & Sand Table — flowing sand three ways
  • Slime — stretch it, squish it, it remembers
  • Scratch — scratch-to-reveal tickets, points just for fun
  • Button Panel — switches, knobs, and lamps that click just right
  • Microphone — rub the grille for that deep, cozy rumble
  • Theremin, Drum Kit & Xylophone — instruments you can't play wrong
  • Soft Serve — pull the lever, catch the swirl, don't drop the cone
