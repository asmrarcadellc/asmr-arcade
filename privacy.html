Privacy Policy

ASMR Arcade · Effective July 3, 2026

The short version

ASMR Arcade collects no data. There are no accounts, no tracking, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs that phone home. Nothing you do in the app leaves your device.

What stays on your device

Your settings (sound and haptic strength), gentle play stats (like bubbles popped and minutes relaxed), and your purchase state are stored only on your device using Apple's standard local storage. We never see them.

Purchases

The one-time "Full Arcade" unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive no personal information about you from the transaction. Purchase records and restores are handled by your Apple ID via Apple's StoreKit.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because we collect nothing from anyone, we also collect nothing from children.

Changes & contact

If a future feature ever changes this policy (for example, optional downloadable sound packs would fetch files from a server without collecting anything), we will update this page before shipping it.
